Lasagna Bolognese

Credit: Serious Eats / Vicky Wasik

Description

This is a full throttle lasagna recipe that would make your Nona cry. Packed with lots of protein and love (you'll need it to commit an afternoon to cooking this monster), perhaps even blood, sweat, and tears. Pull out all the stops and make a meal your guests will never forget (nor will you for that matter).

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter (2 ounces; 60 g), plus more for greasing baking dish
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour (1.25 ounces; 35 g)
  • 3 cups (700 ml) whole milk
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Pinch freshly grated nutmeg
  • 2 pounds (900 g) fresh homemade plain egg or spinach pasta or store-bought fresh lasagna noodles, or 2 (450 g) boxes dry lasagna noodles
  • Vegetable oil, for greasing pasta
  • 1 1/2 quarts (1.5 L) warm ragù bolognese
  • 3 ounces (85 g) Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
